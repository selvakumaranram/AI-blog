A new project called ESPectre utilizes Wi-Fi signal analysis to identify movement within a space. By monitoring fluctuations in wireless spectrum data, the system can detect activity without the need for traditional cameras or infrared sensors. This open-source implementation runs on standard hardware to provide a privacy-conscious alternative for motion sensing.

**Why it matters:** This approach demonstrates how environmental signal processing can replace visual monitoring, offering significant implications for privacy-preserving smart home automation and security.
