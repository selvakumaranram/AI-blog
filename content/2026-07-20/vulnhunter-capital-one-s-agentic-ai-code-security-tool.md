Capital One has introduced an autonomous software tool named VulnHunter designed to identify security vulnerabilities within codebases. This agentic system leverages artificial intelligence to proactively scan for potential weaknesses during the development lifecycle. The initiative reflects the organization's ongoing efforts to integrate automated security measures into its engineering workflows.

**Why it matters:** This development highlights the growing trend of utilizing agentic AI to automate complex cybersecurity tasks, potentially reducing the manual burden on developers. It serves as a practical example of how large enterprises are deploying specialized AI agents to bolster internal software integrity.
