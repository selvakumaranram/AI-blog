Public sentiment reveals a growing unease regarding the rapid integration of artificial intelligence into daily life. Many individuals feel alienated by these technologies, yet those responsible for their deployment remain largely unbothered by these concerns. This disconnect highlights a significant friction between the creators of AI systems and the populations they impact.

**Why it matters:** Understanding the widening gap between tech developers and the public is essential for grasping the future of AI adoption and potential regulatory hurdles.
