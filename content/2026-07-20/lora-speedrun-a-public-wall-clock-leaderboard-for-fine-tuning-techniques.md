A new public leaderboard has been launched to track the performance of various fine-tuning methods for AI models. This platform focuses on measuring the actual time required to complete training tasks across different configurations. Developers can now compare the efficiency of their optimization techniques against a standardized set of benchmarks.

**Why it matters:** This resource provides much-needed transparency for practitioners looking to minimize hardware costs and training duration when adapting models. It serves as a practical guide for identifying the fastest workflows in an increasingly crowded ecosystem of fine-tuning strategies.
