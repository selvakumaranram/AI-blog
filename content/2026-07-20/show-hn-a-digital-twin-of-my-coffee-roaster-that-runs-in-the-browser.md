A developer has created a browser-based digital replica of their personal coffee roasting machine. This virtual model allows users to visualize and interact with the roaster's operations directly within a web interface. The project demonstrates how physical hardware can be mirrored in a digital environment for monitoring and simulation purposes.

**Why it matters:** This project highlights the growing accessibility of digital twin technology for hobbyist hardware projects and real-time system monitoring.
