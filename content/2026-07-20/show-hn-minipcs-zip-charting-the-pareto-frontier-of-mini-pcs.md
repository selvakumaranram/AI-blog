A new web resource called MiniPCs.zip has been launched to help users identify the most efficient compact computer hardware. The platform organizes various devices based on their performance metrics relative to cost and power consumption. This project aims to simplify the selection process for enthusiasts looking to optimize their personal computing setups.

**Why it matters:** Efficient hardware is essential for running local AI models and development environments effectively. This tool provides a data-driven approach to finding cost-effective machines capable of handling modern computational workloads.
