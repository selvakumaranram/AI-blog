Investors who previously focused on funding graphics processing units are shifting their attention toward specialized hardware for running AI models. This transition is highlighted by a recent financial agreement involving four hundred million dollars in debt secured by inference-focused silicon. The move suggests a broader evolution in how capital is being deployed to support the growing demand for AI computing capacity.

**Why it matters:** This shift indicates that the market is maturing beyond raw training hardware and is now prioritizing the infrastructure needed for the efficient deployment and operation of AI applications.
