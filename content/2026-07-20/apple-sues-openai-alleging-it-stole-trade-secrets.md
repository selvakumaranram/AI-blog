Apple has initiated legal action against OpenAI regarding the alleged misappropriation of proprietary technical information. The lawsuit claims that the artificial intelligence firm improperly obtained sensitive trade secrets belonging to the tech giant. This development marks a significant escalation in the ongoing competition between major technology companies within the AI sector.

**Why it matters:** This litigation highlights the intensifying legal scrutiny surrounding data practices and intellectual property rights in the rapid development of large language models. It could set a major precedent for how AI companies source their training data and internal technologies.
