This approach utilizes a collaborative group of AI entities to handle specific tasks within the build pipeline. The project highlights how autonomous agents can be coordinated to execute technical workflows traditionally managed by human engineers.

**Why it matters:** This experiment demonstrates the growing capability of AI swarms to perform intricate, multi-step software engineering tasks that require high precision. It offers a glimpse into how autonomous agent architectures could eventually streamline complex infrastructure development.
