Google has implemented restrictions preventing Meta from utilizing its Gemini artificial intelligence models for internal operations. This decision highlights the growing competitive friction between major technology firms as they vie for dominance in the generative AI sector. The move effectively blocks one of the largest industry players from integrating Google's advanced machine learning technology into its own development workflows.

**Why it matters:** This development underscores the escalating rivalry between tech giants and signals a shift toward more guarded control over proprietary AI infrastructure. It suggests that companies are increasingly prioritizing competitive advantage over cross-platform collaboration.
