Google has announced that its command-line interface for Gemini will be officially retired in mid-2026. Developers who currently rely on this specific tool for terminal-based interactions will need to migrate their workflows before the June 18 deadline. This change marks a shift in how the company supports direct programmatic access to its language models.

**Why it matters:** Users who integrate Gemini directly into their development environments must prepare for this transition to avoid service disruptions. It signals a potential consolidation of Google's developer tools as they refine their AI ecosystem.
