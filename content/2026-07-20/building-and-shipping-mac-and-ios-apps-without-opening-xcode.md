This approach utilizes alternative workflows to manage the software lifecycle for Mac and iOS devices. These techniques offer a way to streamline the development process outside of Apple's primary integrated environment.

**Why it matters:** This shift highlights a growing trend toward modular development environments that could eventually integrate AI-driven coding assistants more seamlessly than traditional, rigid IDEs.
