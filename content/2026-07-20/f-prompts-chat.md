A popular repository formerly known as Awesome ChatGPT Prompts has rebranded to f/prompts.chat. This platform serves as a collaborative hub where users can exchange and organize various instructions for large language models. The project remains accessible as an open-source tool that allows teams to host their own private instances.

**Why it matters:** This resource provides a practical way for users to improve their interaction with AI models while maintaining data privacy through self-hosting capabilities.
