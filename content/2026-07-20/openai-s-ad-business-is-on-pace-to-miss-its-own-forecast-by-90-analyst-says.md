Recent financial projections suggest that OpenAI's advertising revenue is significantly underperforming compared to internal expectations. Market observers indicate that the company is currently falling short of its original fiscal targets by a substantial margin. This discrepancy highlights potential challenges in the firm's efforts to diversify its income streams beyond subscription services.

**Why it matters:** This shortfall raises questions about the sustainability of aggressive revenue growth strategies for major AI developers. It also signals that monetizing generative AI through advertising models may be more difficult than initial industry estimates suggested.
