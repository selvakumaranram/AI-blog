Google has officially rebranded its research-focused note-taking application to Gemini Notebook. This update aligns the service more closely with the company's primary artificial intelligence branding. Users can expect the platform to continue offering features centered on document analysis and information synthesis.

**Why it matters:** This shift signals Google's intent to consolidate its diverse AI offerings under a unified brand identity to improve user recognition. It highlights the growing importance of specialized research tools within the broader Gemini ecosystem.
