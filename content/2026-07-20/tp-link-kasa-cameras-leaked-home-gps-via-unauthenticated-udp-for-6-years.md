A significant security vulnerability in TP-Link Kasa smart cameras allowed unauthorized access to precise location data. For over half a decade, these devices broadcasted GPS coordinates through an unencrypted network protocol. This oversight exposed the private whereabouts of users to anyone capable of intercepting the local traffic.

**Why it matters:** This incident highlights the critical security risks inherent in IoT devices, which are increasingly being integrated with AI-driven smart home automation systems.
