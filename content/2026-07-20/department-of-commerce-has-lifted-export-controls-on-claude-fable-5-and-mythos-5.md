The United States Department of Commerce has officially removed trade restrictions previously placed on the Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5 models. This regulatory shift allows for the unrestricted international distribution of these specific artificial intelligence systems. These models are now cleared for global export following the government's decision to rescind the prior limitations.

**Why it matters:** This policy change significantly broadens the market reach for these advanced AI tools, potentially accelerating their adoption across international borders. It also highlights the evolving nature of government oversight regarding the global proliferation of high-performance machine learning technology.
