Anthropic's chief executive has expressed growing apprehension regarding the widespread distribution of unrestricted artificial intelligence models. The leadership argues that providing open access to powerful underlying technology poses significant security risks. This perspective highlights a fundamental tension between the movement for transparent software and the potential for misuse in dangerous applications.

**Why it matters:** This debate underscores the ongoing industry struggle to balance innovation with safety, influencing how future regulations might restrict access to foundational AI models.
