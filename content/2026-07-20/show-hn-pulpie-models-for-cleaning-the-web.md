Pulpie has been introduced as a specialized suite of machine learning models designed to refine and sanitize web-based data. The project focuses on improving the quality of information extracted from online sources for various downstream tasks. Developers can now utilize these tools to streamline their data preparation workflows.

**Why it matters:** High-quality datasets are essential for training effective AI systems, and these models offer a practical solution for filtering out digital noise. This release provides a valuable resource for engineers looking to enhance the reliability of their web-scraped content.
