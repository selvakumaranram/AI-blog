Apple has reportedly initiated legal communication directed at numerous staff members currently employed by OpenAI. These formal notices appear to be part of an effort to address concerns regarding personnel movement between the two tech giants. The company is actively scrutinizing the transition of talent as it seeks to bolster its own internal artificial intelligence initiatives.

**Why it matters:** This move highlights the intensifying competition for top-tier AI talent as major technology firms vie for dominance in the rapidly evolving sector.
