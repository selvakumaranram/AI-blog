Former Federal Reserve Chair Ben Bernanke has been appointed to the board of Anthropic's Long-Term Benefit Trust. This organization is tasked with overseeing the company's commitment to safety and ethical development. His inclusion brings significant institutional experience to the governance structure of the AI developer.

**Why it matters:** The appointment highlights a growing trend of integrating high-level economic and policy expertise into the oversight frameworks of major artificial intelligence firms.
