A new open-source project called Rowboat has been introduced as a self-hosted substitute for the official Claude desktop application. This software focuses on a local-first architecture to provide users with more control over their AI interactions. It is currently being shared with the developer community for feedback and collaborative improvement.

**Why it matters:** This tool offers a privacy-conscious alternative for those who prefer running their AI interfaces locally rather than relying on proprietary cloud-based clients.
