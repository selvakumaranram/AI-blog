Recent data visualization reveals a significant decline in user activity on Stack Overflow following the widespread adoption of generative AI tools. The analysis highlights how developers are increasingly turning to automated assistants rather than traditional community forums to resolve technical queries. This shift suggests a fundamental transformation in how programmers seek and share knowledge online.

**Why it matters:** This trend underscores the disruptive impact of LLMs on established knowledge-sharing ecosystems and signals a potential long-term decline for legacy developer platforms.
