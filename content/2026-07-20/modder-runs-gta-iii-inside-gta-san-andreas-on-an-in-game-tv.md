A creative developer successfully integrated the classic game Grand Theft Auto III into the environment of Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas. The project functions by rendering the older title directly onto a television screen within the game world. This technical achievement demonstrates a unique method of nested software execution within a pre-existing game engine.
