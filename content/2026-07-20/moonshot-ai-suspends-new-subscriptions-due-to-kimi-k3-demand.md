Moonshot AI has temporarily halted the registration of new users for its Kimi platform. This decision follows an overwhelming surge in interest for the company's latest K3 model. The organization is currently managing capacity constraints to maintain service stability for existing clients.

**Why it matters:** This development highlights the intense competition and infrastructure strain currently facing leading generative AI startups as they scale to meet massive public demand.
