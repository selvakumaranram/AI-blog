Apple has initiated legal proceedings against OpenAI, accusing the startup of misappropriating proprietary trade secrets. The litigation highlights concerns regarding the significant number of former Apple staff currently employed by the AI firm, including high-ranking executives. OpenAI has issued a guarded statement while facing this challenge during a period of intense scrutiny.

**Why it matters:** This legal conflict could significantly impact OpenAI's operational stability and future financial plans, such as its rumored public offering. It also underscores the growing tension between legacy tech giants and emerging AI developers over talent acquisition and intellectual property.
