Airbus has officially decided to move away from Amazon Web Services for its cloud infrastructure requirements. This strategic shift marks a significant departure from their previous reliance on the tech giant's hosting solutions. The aerospace firm is now transitioning its operations to alternative platforms.

**Why it matters:** This move highlights the growing trend of major enterprises reevaluating their cloud dependencies, which could impact the broader ecosystem of AI development and data storage services.
