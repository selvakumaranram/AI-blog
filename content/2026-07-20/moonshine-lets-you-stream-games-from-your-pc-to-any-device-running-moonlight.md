Moonshine has been introduced as a new solution for broadcasting PC gaming experiences to various hardware platforms. It functions as a compatible host for the existing Moonlight client software. This development offers users an alternative method for remote play across their personal devices.

**Why it matters:** This project highlights the ongoing evolution of low-latency streaming infrastructure, which is a critical component for the future of cloud-based gaming and remote computing environments.
