OpenAI has implemented a reduction in the maximum context window for its Codex model. The capacity has been adjusted downward from 372,000 tokens to 272,000 tokens. This change impacts the amount of information the system can process at once during programming tasks.

**Why it matters:** Developers relying on Codex for large-scale codebases may need to adjust their workflows to accommodate the smaller input limit. It serves as a reminder of the evolving nature of model constraints in production environments.
