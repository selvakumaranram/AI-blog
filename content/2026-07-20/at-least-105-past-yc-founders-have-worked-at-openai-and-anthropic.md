Data reveals that over one hundred individuals who previously established companies through Y Combinator have taken positions at major artificial intelligence firms like OpenAI and Anthropic. This trend highlights a significant migration of entrepreneurial talent toward the leading players in the generative AI sector. The findings underscore how startup founders are increasingly prioritizing roles within established AI labs over launching their own independent ventures.

**Why it matters:** This movement of experienced founders into top AI companies suggests that the most influential talent is consolidating within a few dominant organizations, potentially shaping the future trajectory of the industry.
