Valve has indicated that the current shortage of memory components shows no signs of abating soon. Consequently, consumers should anticipate rising costs for hardware that relies on these specific parts. The company suggests that market pressures will continue to drive price hikes for the foreseeable future.

**Why it matters:** Rising memory costs directly impact the affordability of high-performance computing hardware essential for training and running large-scale AI models. This trend could increase the financial barrier to entry for developers and researchers building local AI infrastructure.
