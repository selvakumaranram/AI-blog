Google has introduced new computer-use capabilities for its Gemini 3.5 Flash model. This update allows the artificial intelligence to interact directly with desktop interfaces to perform tasks. The system is designed to navigate software environments and execute commands on behalf of the user.

**Why it matters:** This advancement signals a shift toward AI agents that can actively manipulate operating systems rather than just processing text or code. It represents a significant step in automating complex workflows across various desktop applications.
