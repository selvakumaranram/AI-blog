Security researchers have identified a vulnerability involving the potential exposure of session data between separate workspace environments or individual user profiles. This flaw suggests that private information could inadvertently cross boundaries when multiple instances are active. Developers are currently investigating the underlying architecture to prevent unauthorized access to sensitive account details.

**Why it matters:** This issue highlights critical security risks in multi-tenant AI platforms where data isolation is essential for maintaining user privacy and enterprise compliance.
