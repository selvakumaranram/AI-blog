A developer recently introduced Leaves, a command-line utility designed to visualize storage consumption through a treemap interface. This tool operates directly within the terminal to provide users with an intuitive spatial breakdown of their file systems. It aims to simplify the process of identifying large directories and managing disk space efficiently.

**Why it matters:** Efficient data management is essential for developers training large models or handling massive datasets, and terminal-based tools offer a lightweight alternative to resource-heavy graphical interfaces.
