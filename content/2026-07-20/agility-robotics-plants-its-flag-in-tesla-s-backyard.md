This site is situated in Fremont, California, placing the company in close proximity to major automotive manufacturing hubs. The expansion focuses on scaling the operational capabilities of the Digit robotic platform.

**Why it matters:** The move signals an intensifying competition in the humanoid robotics sector as companies vie for dominance in industrial automation.
