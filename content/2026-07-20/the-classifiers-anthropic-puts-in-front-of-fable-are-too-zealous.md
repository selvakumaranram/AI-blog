Users have reported that Anthropic's safety filters are overly restrictive when interacting with the Fable platform. These automated guardrails frequently trigger false positives that prevent legitimate tasks from being completed. Consequently, the platform's utility is being hampered by these aggressive moderation settings.

**Why it matters:** This situation highlights the ongoing tension between implementing robust AI safety measures and maintaining a functional, user-friendly experience.
