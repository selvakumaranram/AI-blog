Google has announced that its Gemini Code Assist service is scheduled to be discontinued on July 17. Users of the platform will lose access to the tool on this specific date. No further information regarding a direct replacement or a migration path was provided in the initial notification.

**Why it matters:** Developers relying on this integration for their daily workflows must now identify and transition to alternative coding assistants before the service goes offline.
