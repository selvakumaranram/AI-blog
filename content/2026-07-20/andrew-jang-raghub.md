A new repository named RAGHub has been launched to aggregate various projects and frameworks related to Retrieval-Augmented Generation. This platform functions as a collaborative space where developers can share and investigate different components of the RAG landscape. The initiative aims to centralize technical resources to help users navigate the rapidly changing field of augmented language models.
