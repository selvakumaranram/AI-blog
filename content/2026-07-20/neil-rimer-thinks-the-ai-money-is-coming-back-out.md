Index Ventures co-founder Neil Rimer suggests that the immense capital currently flowing into artificial intelligence will eventually need to be dispersed. He anticipates that this massive accumulation of wealth within the tech sector will face pressure to be shared more broadly. This redistribution process could occur through either proactive corporate choices or external regulatory mandates.

**Why it matters:** This perspective highlights a growing concern among venture capitalists regarding the long-term economic sustainability and social impact of the current AI investment boom.
