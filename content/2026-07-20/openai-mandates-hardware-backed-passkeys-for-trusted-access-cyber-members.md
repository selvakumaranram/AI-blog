OpenAI has introduced a requirement for members of its Trusted Access Cyber program to utilize hardware-based passkeys. This update aims to bolster security protocols for users accessing sensitive development environments. The shift moves away from traditional password-based authentication to more robust physical security measures.

**Why it matters:** As AI companies become prime targets for cyberattacks, this move signals a broader industry trend toward adopting hardware-level security to protect proprietary models and data.
