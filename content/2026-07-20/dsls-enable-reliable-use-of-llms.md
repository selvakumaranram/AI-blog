Developers are increasingly turning to domain-specific languages to improve the consistency of large language model outputs. These specialized frameworks impose structured constraints on how models generate information. By restricting the scope of interaction, these tools help reduce errors in automated workflows.

**Why it matters:** Adopting structured language approaches provides a path toward making AI systems more predictable and dependable for enterprise applications.
