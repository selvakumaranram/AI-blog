Patreon has initiated a more aggressive strategy to prevent artificial intelligence developers from harvesting data from its platform. By partnering with Cloudflare, the company is now actively restricting automated scrapers rather than simply requesting they stay away. This transition moves beyond passive site instructions to enforce strict technical barriers against unauthorized model training.

**Why it matters:** This shift highlights a growing trend where platforms are taking direct technical action to protect creator intellectual property from being exploited by AI companies. It signals a move toward more robust enforcement mechanisms in the ongoing conflict between content hosts and data scrapers.
