A recent study indicates that individuals relying on artificial intelligence recommendations experienced a decrease in task accuracy. Despite the decline in performance, participants reported a significantly higher level of certainty in their incorrect decisions. These findings highlight a potential psychological trap where automated assistance breeds misplaced trust.

**Why it matters:** This research underscores the critical need for human oversight and skepticism when integrating AI into decision-making workflows to avoid automation bias.
