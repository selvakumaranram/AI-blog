Users on Hacker News are expressing strong opposition to the potential removal of the Gemini 2.5 Flash model from Google's service offerings. The community discussion highlights a significant reliance on this specific iteration for various development tasks. Participants are urging the company to maintain access to this version rather than forcing a migration to newer alternatives.

**Why it matters:** This feedback underscores how developers prioritize model stability and consistent performance over the constant introduction of newer, untested iterations. It serves as a reminder that abrupt changes to API availability can disrupt established workflows for many technical users.
